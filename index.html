Top view

Left mouse button - Select / deselect model

Right mouse button - Select two points for transilation

R - Enable / disable rotation

S - Enable / disable scaling

Middle mouse button - Hold and drag mouse to scale or rotate

X - Change rotation axis to x

Y - Change rotation axis to y

Z - Change rotation axis to z

Space - Change rotation axis to arbitrary

3D view

Middle mouse button - Hold and drag mouse to rotate camera

X - Change rotation axis to x

Y - Change rotation axis to y

Z - Change rotation axis to z

Space - Change rotation axis to arbitrary

Tab - Toggle view
